# Jashari-Residence
3D VR Visualization of the Jashari Residence, for an enhanced educational experience. We will narrate it, educating people about the details they might not know.
Adem Jashari is one of the most revered figures in Albanian and Kosovar history a symbol of
resistance, sacrifice, and national identity. His home in Prekaz, Kosovo, stands today as a
memorial to his life and to the legendary stand he and his family made in March 1998, when
they defended their compound against overwhelming Serbian forces for four days. That house,
scarred by battle and preserved as a national monument, carries the weight of an entire
people's struggle for freedom.

This project proposes the development of an immersive Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented
Reality (AR) experience that digitally reconstructs Adem Jashari's house as it stands today
allowing users to walk through a faithful 3D recreation of this historically and culturally significant
site. Rather than reading about history or viewing static photographs, users will be transported
into the space itself, experiencing its architecture, atmosphere, and emotional weight firsthand.

Built in Blender for high-fidelity 3D modeling and optimized in Unreal Engine 5 for real-time
VR/AR performance, this experience bridges the gap between cultural heritage preservation
and cutting-edge immersive technology. It is both a tribute to Albanian history and a
demonstration of how modern tools can breathe life into memory.
